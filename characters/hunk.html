§ CONCEPT

Hunk started off as a mere random blob on a testing canvas, with his only remarkable feature being his crooked winking face which has managed to stick through multiple iterations of his design. One of my friends saw the drawing and called him a "hunky boy", and that is where he got his name from.
Yep, from here on out, it only got grander.

In Hunk Quest, nothing is really consistent aside from its characters wearing shoes with long tongues. Everything originates from a mishmash of random doodles, inspirations and out-of-left-field improvisation with a ton of evergrowing love for Drum and Bass on the side.
