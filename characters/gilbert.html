Gil "Gilbert" Senden is a cosmic stuntman constantly dodging spikes and sawblades. Whether it is by using pogo sticks, jetpacks or even his sweet kicks, Gilbert is always knowledgeable on how to move around any obstacle course. He is just the coolest guy to be
around, overall.

§ CONCEPT

Back when dnb was in a very early phase, the player was originally decipted as a humanoid silhouette moving through outlined levels with a basic outer space background.

One of the test footages for showcasing dnb's gameplay flow also had south.vibe's "vague [24.12]" playing in the background. This track - along with the early atmosphere - greatly influenced Gilbert's final, laid-back stickman design.
