Video and her colleagues are famous among HAWK workers for having particularly sharp senses in extremely hazardous environments.
She is an intrepid cyborg from the Mech Maintenance Team with
a special exosuit that lets her navigate around sites and
carry all things heavy with ease.

§ CONCEPT

Video was originally a draft made for practicing character designing on the fly. Her initial design was inspired by Monster World IV, Mischief Makers, Xbox controllers and Megaman's proportions. Even though the result looked cheesy, it eventually grew on me anyway, and I ended up revising her design with a more stylized look.

For a futuristic themed puzzle game like Auger Bolt, Video and Co fit in pretty well as characters. It's just the matter of execution that screws the final bolt on the mech.
