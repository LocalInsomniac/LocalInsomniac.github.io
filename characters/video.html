§ CONCEPT

Video was originally a draft made for practicing character designing on the fly; in this case, it was tech-themed. Her initial design was inspired by Monster World IV, Mischief Makers, Xbox controllers and Megaman's proportions. Even though the result looked rather silly, it eventually grew on me anyway, and I ended up revising her design with a more stylized look.

For a futuristic-themed puzzle game like Auger Bolt, Video fits in pretty well as a character. It is just the matter of execution that will truly screw the final bolt on the mech.

§ TRIVIA

  • Video's forearms are modular. She can spin them around like drills.
  • Video can activate her exosuit's eye-shaped flashlights by tapping her left foot on the ground twice. They form a triclops pattern in the dark.
  • Video has a habit of haphazardly carrying things above her head, including her colleagues.
