§ CONCEPT

Video was originally a draft made for practicing character designs on the fly. Her design was inspired by Monster World IV, Mischief Makers and Xbox controllers. Even though the result looked cheesy, it eventually grew on me anyway, and I ended up finalizing her design into its current form.

Meanwhile, PNEngine was being developed and it needed an original project that could test its might. So, I decided to forge Auger Bolt.
