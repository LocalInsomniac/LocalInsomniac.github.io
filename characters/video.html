§ CONCEPT

Video was originally a draft made for practicing character designing on the fly; in this case, it was tech-themed. Her initial design was based on Megaman-style proportions, Mischief Makers and Xbox controllers. Even though it was as generic as it seemed, it eventually grew on me anyway, and I ended up revising her design with a more stylized look.

For a futuristic-themed puzzle platformer like Auger Bolt, Video would make a pretty good candidate. After that, it's only the matter of execution that will truly screw the final bolt on the mech.
