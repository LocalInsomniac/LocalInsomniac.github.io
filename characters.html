HUNK
Hunk Quest | Schwungus Software

BIO

Nobody really knows where Hunk came from. All that's known is that ever since he appeared, his taller, lankier counterpart always comes around causing trouble across Dollop. Being the perfect candidate in liftocracy, Hunk is the only guy who has more than enough muscle to stop Lank, and you'd best hope you're not standing in his way.

TRIVIA

Hunk literally started off as a random blob on a testing canvas. One of my friends saw the drawing and called him a "hunky boy", and that's where he got his name from. Hunk and his environment are basically a mishmash of out-of-left-field improvisation and an evergrowing love towards Drum and Bass.

GILBERT
dnb | NO5

BIO

Gil “Gilbert” Senden is a cosmic stuntman who's always dodging spikes and sawblades. Whether it's by using pogo sticks, jetpacks or even his sweet kicks, Gilbert is always knowledgeable on how to move around any obstacle course. He's just the coolest guy to be around, overall.

TRIVIA

Back when dnb was in a very early phase, the player was originally a humanoid silhouette moving through outlined levels with a basic outer space background. In this clip, south.vibe's "vague [24.12]" was playing in the background. Alongside the test footage's atmosphere, this greatly influenced Gilbert's final, laid-back stickman design:



Concept art of Gilbert in dynamic poses


dnb is on an indefinite hiatus at the time of writing this, but regardless of that I still had a fun time helping out on its development.
