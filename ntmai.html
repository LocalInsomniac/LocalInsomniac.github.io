DOOM FROM AN ALTERNATE UNIVERSE

Nobody Told Me About id is a love letter from DOOM fans to the iconic trinity of Build Engine games; Duke Nukem 3D, Blood and Shadow Warrior. Whether it be ludicrous gore or explosions, NTMAi tries its best to faithfully replicate all things Build on DOOM while also enhancing the base gameplay. Your arsenal may be tough, but some enemies will have a trick or two up their sleeves...

NTMAi originally started off as a small project during summer 2019. It went on to win a Cacoward in 2020 and eventually got rebooted to a much better state in early 2023.





HURT ME PLENTY MORE

Want some extra challenge? NTMAi also comes with built-in special support for a select few map packs such as AD MORTEM, MAYhem 2019 and Scythe 2. Playing on these will automatically enable badder monsters and maybe even some new weapons to play with!
