DOOM FROM AN ALTERNATE UNIVERSE

Nobody Told Me About id is a love letter from DOOM fans to the iconic trinity of Build Engine games; Duke Nukem 3D, Blood and Shadow Warrior. Whether it be ludicrous gibs or explosions, NTMAi tries its best to faithfully replicate all things Build on DOOM while also enhancing vanilla gameplay. Your arsenal may be tough, but some enemies will have a trick or two up their sleeves...

NTMAi originally started off as a small project during summer 2019. It went on to win a Cacoward in 2020 and eventually got rebooted to a much better state in early 2023.

HURT ME PLENTY MORE

Want some extra challenge? NTMAi also comes with built-in special support for the following WADs:
  • AD MORTEM
  • AUGER;ZENITH
  • DOOM 64 for DOOM II
  • Eviternity
  • MAYhem 2019
  • Scythe 2
Playing these will automatically enable unique content; maybe even some new weapons and monsters to play with!

For maximum compatibility, make sure to load NTMAi after any of these WADs, otherwise you might get weird results.
